Born and raised in Georgia, Drea Salgado has been singing for as long as she can remember. What began as a lifelong love for music has grown into a powerful creative journey, as she now steps fully into her voice as both a singer and songwriter.

Rooted in the South, Drea shapes a sound that blends the raw, heartfelt emotion of soul with the honest storytelling of Country Music.

She has been steadily building her presence across Georgia, gigging in intimate coffee shops, lively breweries, and most recently performing at the Suwannee Beer Festival.

With every show and every song, Drea Salgado continues to carve out a space where soul meets, where stories matter, emotions run deep, and the music always feels like home.
